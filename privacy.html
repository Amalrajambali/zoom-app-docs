Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information when using this Zoom integration.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to provide Zoom meeting-related functionality, including:

  • Zoom user ID
  • Meeting details such as meeting ID, topic, date, and time
  • Authorization tokens provided by Zoom

How We Use Information

The collected information is used only to:

  • Create and manage Zoom meetings
  • Enable authorized Zoom features requested by the user

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Data is shared only with Zoom as required to provide this integration.

Data Retention

User data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service and is deleted when no longer required.

Users have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of their data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@yourcompany.com

Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or disclosure.
